Ingredients:
2 slices of bread
Peanut Butter
Jelly

Directions:
Using a kitchen knife, spread generous amounts of penaut butter on one side of each slice of bread
Lick any excess peanut butter off the knife without cutting your tongue
Squirt some jelly on to the penute buttered side of one slice of bread.
Spread it with the knife. (If this sandwich is NOT for you, wash the knife first)
Put the non-jelly slice of bread on top of the other, peanut butter side down.
Press down gently but firmly on the sandwich.
If this is your sandwich, lick the jelly and peanut butter that has squirted out the sides. (Keep it over the plate as you do this)

Enjoy!!
